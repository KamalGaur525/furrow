FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  • What makes Furrow tractors different from other brands?

    Furrow is a trusted machinery brand, delivering excellence in agricultural solutions under one roof - Tillage, Sowing, Harvesting & Plant Protection. We are passionate about revolutionizing agriculture with innovative, durable and field-tested machinery. Our implements are engineered to empower farmers with tools that respect the soil and increase profitability, combining traditional farming wisdom with cutting-edge technology.

  • Where are Furrow products manufactured?

    Furrow products are manufactured in Rajasthan, India with state-of-the-art facilities ensuring the highest quality standards. Our manufacturing units are equipped with modern technology and skilled workforce dedicated to producing reliable, durable agricultural machinery. We maintain strict quality control measures at every stage of production to deliver excellence to farmers across the country.

  • What kind of after-sales support does Furrow provide?

    At Furrow, we believe in building lasting relationships with our customers. We provide comprehensive after-sales support through our Pan India Support Dealer network. Our services include quality assured supply, timely spare parts availability, technical assistance, and dedicated customer service. We are committed to ensuring that our farmers get maximum value and longevity from their Furrow equipment.

  • How can I become a Furrow dealer?

    We are always looking to expand our dealer network with partners who share our passion for agriculture and commitment to farmer success. If you're interested in becoming a Furrow dealer, please visit our "Become a Dealer" section on our website or contact us directly. We provide comprehensive training, marketing support, and attractive business opportunities to our dealer partners.

  • What is Furrow's commitment to sustainability?

    Furrow is deeply committed to sustainable farming practices. Our machinery is designed to respect the soil, reduce environmental impact, and promote long-term agricultural sustainability. We focus on developing implements that help farmers achieve better yields while preserving natural resources. Our goal is to empower farmers with tools that not only increase productivity but also contribute to a healthier planet for future generations.
